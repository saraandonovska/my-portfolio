Arts

I've been drawing since I was a child—it's always felt like the most natural way for me to express myself. Every drawing I create carries a specific meaning, whether it's tied to a memory, an emotion, or something that inspires me in the world around me. I don’t just draw what I see, I draw what I feel. Art has always been more than a hobby for me; it's a personal language, a space where I can translate thoughts and experiences into something visual. The inspiration never really stops—it's in people, nature, music, and even silence.

Sports

Sports are a big part of my everyday life, they keep me energized, focused, and happy. Whether I’m training, hiking, or just moving, staying active is something I genuinely enjoy and do regularly. It’s not just about fitness for me, it’s about the feeling of freedom and challenge. I also love combining sports with social time, hanging out with friends, sharing the experience, and pushing each other to do better. It’s my way of staying balanced and connected, both physically and mentally.

Traveling

Traveling is one of my biggest passions, it’s how I learn, grow, and connect with the world. I love discovering new cultures, meeting people with different perspectives, and finding inspiration in every place I visit. I've had the chance to be part of several Erasmus projects, including amazing experiences in Paris and Italy, which shaped the way I see both myself and others. Whether I’m traveling with friends or family, I always enjoy the journey. I especially love summer activities, beaches, outdoor events, late-night walks—everything feels alive and full of possibility when I’m exploring somewhere new.
